About

My name is Sophia and I study Information Science at Cornell University, concentrating in User Experience and minoring in Game Design.

I was the 135th Editor in Chief of The Cornell Daily Sun, out of a passion for writing and for my fellow Cornellians. I have been a Teaching Assistant for Introductory Web Design at Cornell for the past two years, guiding students new to the tech and design industries. This summer, I am a software engineering intern at Microsoft. I've struggled to give myself a label for what exactly I am, but these experiences have made me realize that I simply enjoy working across creative and technical disciplines to empower others and solve interesting problems.

In my free time, catch me obsessing over coffee with oat milk, cool artists on Instagram, and my Wacom tablet :)
